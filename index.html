GET TO KNOW US

Taekwondo – India’s Most Popular Martial Art

Taekwondo is the most popular martial art in our country and is a regular event in the Olympic Games, Asian Games, and National Games.

The Taekwondo Federation of India (TFI) is the official national governing body for taekwondo in India, established in 1976 and recognized by the Indian Olympic Association.
