IMPORTANT NOTICE: Court Judgment

Legal Update from the Hon'ble High Court of Delhi

Back to News
November 21, 2025 1 min read Legal Update

With this historic judgment, it is now reaffirmed that TFI is the one and only National Sports Federation for Taekwondo in India.

It is a proud moment for all of us. The Hon'ble High Court of Delhi has released its order against the MYAS recognition given to INDIA TAEKWONDO (IT). clearly stating that India Taekwondo is not at all a federation to be recognized by the ministry of youth affairs, Government of India. The Court has also strongly reprimanded MYAS for granting such recognition.
