About the Event

The 8th National Cadet Kyorugi & Poomsae Championships is a premier national-level Taekwondo event organized under the aegis of the Taekwondo Federation of India (TFI). This event is dedicated to cadet-level athletes (ages 12-14), encouraging young talent to compete and improve their technical and tactical skills in both sparring (Kyorugi) and forms (Poomsae). The championship serves as a talent identification platform for future national champions.
